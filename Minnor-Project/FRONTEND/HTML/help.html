Help & Support

Welcome to our online book exchange! We believe every book deserves a new reader. Here, you can give your books a second life by selling, donating, or swapping them with others in your community — all with just a few clicks.

Our mission is to make books more accessible and sustainable by creating a platform where readers can exchange, sell, or donate books safely. We aim to build a community of book lovers who share knowledge while reducing waste.

  • How to upload my book?

    Go to “Upload Book” and fill in the details with clear photos.

  • How to exchange my book?

    1. Browse the book you want
    2. Select your book to offer
    3. Click "Send Exchange Request"
    4. Chat with the user
    5. Meet and exchange the books
  • How to create an account?
    Click on 'Sign Up' → fill in your details →click on sign up button.
  • Is book exchange free?
    Yes, exchanging books is completely free on our platform.
  • How do I contact another user?
    You can message them through the in-platform chat after logging in.
